About Me

Hey, I'm Tim Wang. I am a junior at the University of Michigan's School of Information studying UI/UX Design and Computer Science.

Creating and designing have been lifelong passions of mine, and I strive to create beautiful and efficient designs that bridge the gap between technology and people.

Recently, I have been working on creating designs for a bubble tea mobile-order application. The project is completely self-led.

I wanted to combine two of my interests into a project that would provide a meaningful learning experience about design and marketing. I constantly strive to learn more about design, and I am excited for what's next.
